Frequently Asked Questions

What is Aromic?

Aromic is an architecture and interior design platform that helps you visualize, design, and transform spaces with powerful 3D tools.

How do I create a project?

Tap "Architecture" on the home screen, enter your project details, and start designing your rooms.

Can I export my designs?

Yes! You can export your designs as images or 3D models from the Settings > Export All Designs option.

Is Aromic free?

Aromic offers a free tier with basic features. Premium features are available with a subscription.

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Account > Change Password to update your credentials.

User Guide

1. Getting Started

Create an account, set up your profile, and explore the dashboard.

2. Creating Architecture Projects

Tap Architecture on the home screen to start a new project. Add rooms, floors, and customize dimensions.

3. Using Materials Library

Browse thousands of materials for flooring, walls, furniture, and fixtures. Save your favorites for quick access.

4. Photo Design

Upload photos and apply architectural styles and materials to visualize your designs in real spaces.

5. 3D House View

View your projects in 3D with interactive controls. Toggle walls, floors, and explore every room.
